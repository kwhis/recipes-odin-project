Shrimp and Grits Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup grits
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 6 slices bacon, chopped
  • 4 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp shopped parsley
  • 1 cup sliced scallions
  • 1 minced garlic clove

Directions

  1. Bring water to boil. Add salt and pepper.
  2. Add grits to boiling water. Cook 20-25 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat. Stir in butter and cheese.
  4. Rinse shrimp and pat dry.
  5. Fry bacon in a skillet until browned. Drain grease.
  6. Cook shrimp in grease until pink. Add lemon juice, chopped bacon, parlsey, scallions, and garlic. Saute 3 minutes.
  7. Spook grits into serving bowl. Top with shrimp mixture and mix well.
  8. Serve hot. Enjoy.
