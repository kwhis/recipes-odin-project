Biscuits and Gravy Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 can jumbo biscuits
  • 1 package sausage crumbles
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 1/2 cups milk
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Arrange biscuits on baking sheet and bake 13-15 minutes.
  2. Cook sausage crumbles in skillet on medium heat 5-6 minutes.
  3. Stir in flour and gradually mix in milk. Stir until gravy thickens and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer for 2 minutes while still stirring. Salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Split biscuits and top with gravy.
  6. Serve hot. Enjoy.
