Chicken and Dumplings Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 quart chicken stock
  • 4 cups shredded chicken
  • 1 tbsp thyme
  • 2 tbsp pepper
  • salt to taste

For the Dumplings:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tbsp & 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp thyme
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 4 tbsp melted butter

Directions

  1. Saute diced veggies in butter over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Stir in flour and cook an additional minute.
  4. Mix in milk and stock. Bring to a boil.
  5. Add chicken and seasonings. Simmer uncovered.
  6. In a large bowl, whisk dumpling ingredients until dough forms.
  7. Scoop dough and drop into simmering soup.
  8. Cover and lower heat. Cook 15 minutes.
  9. Serve hot.
